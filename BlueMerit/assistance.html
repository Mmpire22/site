Services/Visa Assistance

VISA ASSISTANCE | DOCUMENTATIONS | VISA SUPPORT

We assist our clients to obtain visas - Our visa types range from short-stay or tourist visas,
business visas, electronic visas, and medical visas

Blue-Merit Travel & Services provides visa assistance, visa support, documentations and we also help our clients to obtain a valid visa to travel to countries around the world, and other visa related services to individuals and corporate clients across industries. We have built a trusted corporate portfolio across embassies and consulates in Nigeria and beyond, and this gives us a kind of unparalleled leverage over the competition. We pursue all those little applications, facilitating and providing advisory services in relation to visa acquisition for and on behalf of clients.
